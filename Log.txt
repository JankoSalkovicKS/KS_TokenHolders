/*
    What's going on here?
*/

    - Stephen's words: 
        1. 'The use case in Keeper Solutions could be the issuing of tokens to employees as a way to provide some ownership in the company'
        2. 'The idea is to provide a stake in the company for team members which corresponds to the contribution that people have made. 
            So, for example we could link say 10% of the company's shares to a set of tokens and then find a way to allocate the tokens
            to team members.  We would need to consider the rules for this, and how they could be allocated.  It might or might not work, 
            but I think it would be a good use case to research'
        3. 'I thought it might be possible for team members to apply for tokens based on their length of stay in the company to date.
            The app might then allocate tokens on an ongoing basis (e.g. 1 additional token for each day worked)
            If the company were sold on a given date, then the value of the company would be split based on the number of tokens held 
            by each team member.

            Then there is the tricky issue of what about people who leave the company.... perhaps they get to hold on to their tokens, 
            but the tokens are gradually diluted as more and more tokens are issued each month'

    - Jankova interpretacija:
        - dijelovi firme bi se dijelili samo ljudima koji su na višim pozicijama, zaduženima za napredak firme i vođenje ljudima
        - uzeti neki postotak vrijednosti firme -> prenijeti ga u neku tokensku protuvrijednost.
        - udjeli u tokenima, pojedinih djelatnika, morali bi se nekako povećavati s vremenom kojeg su oni proveli u firmi
        - ako se firma proda, ovisno o udjelima tokena, svaki od zaposlenika koji posjeduje token, morao bi naslijediti neku novčanu vrijednost
        - što s ljudima koji su imali tokene, a napustili firmu
